About ToolNest

Your trusted partner in quality hardware and tools since 2010

Our Story

ToolNest was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to revolutionize the way people shop for hardware and tools. We began as a small family-owned business with a single location in Portland, Oregon, driven by a passion for exceptional customer service and quality hardware products.

Over the years, we've grown into a nationwide retailer with over 50 locations across the United States, but our core values remain the same. We believe in providing our customers with the perfect combination of premium tools, expert advice, and competitive pricing.

Our founders, James and Sarah Wheeler, both DIY enthusiasts and professional contractors, recognized that finding the right tools and hardware was often overwhelming for customers. They set out to create a more customer-friendly approach, combining traditional hardware expertise with modern technology solutions.
